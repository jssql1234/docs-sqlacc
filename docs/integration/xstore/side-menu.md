## User Profile

The User Profile page displays your account information.

![user-profile](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/user-profile.png)

| Field            | Description                        |
|------------------|------------------------------------|
| **Name**         | Your display name                  |
| **Email**        | Your registered email address      |
| **User ID**      | Unique identifier for your account |
| **Phone Number** | Contact phone number               |

### Update User Profile

1. Click the **Edit Profile** button.

   ![update-user-profile](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/edit-profile-button.png)

2. Update the fields and click **Save**.

   ![update-user-profile-2](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/edit-profile-page.png)

## Passkeys

Manage your registered passkeys for secure authentication.

![passkeys](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/passkey.png)

### Register New Passkey

1. Click **Create Passkey** button.

   ![register-new-passkey](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/register-passkey-button.png)

2. Enter a **Passkey Name** to help you identify this device later.

   ![authentication-prompt](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/register-passkey-dialog.png)

3. Click **Register**, then complete the authentication prompt on your device.

   ![authentication-prompt-2](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/register-passkey-device.png)

### Rename Passkey

1. Click the **More** icon and select **Rename**.

2. Rename your passkey and click **Save**.

   ![rename-passkey](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/rename-passkey.png)

### Revoke Passkey

1. Click the **More** icon and select **Revoke** to remove the passkey immediately.

## Sessions

View your active login sessions across different devices and browsers.

![sessions](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/sessions.png)

### Logout Sessions

1. Click on the session.

   ![sessions-2](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/revoke-sessions.png)

2. Click **Confirm** to revoke.

   ![sessions-2](../../../static/img/xstore/sidemenu/revoke-sessions-dialog.png)
